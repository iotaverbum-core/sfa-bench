FAQ

Is this another LLM grading my LLM?
No. The judge is a deterministic rule verifier, so results are reproducible and can't drift or be gamed by the answer key.
Does my data leave my environment?
No. GroundLedger runs in your VPC with no network egress and no dependencies.
What does "tamper-evident" mean?
Any covered edit to a sealed receipt or the ledger breaks an integrity check that replay reports. It is evidence of tampering, not a cryptographic impossibility proof.
What answers can it check today?
Structured, cited answers (JSON-mode / function-calling RAG). Free-text claim extraction is on the roadmap.
