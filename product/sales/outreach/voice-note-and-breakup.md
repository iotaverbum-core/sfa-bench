# Founder voice note + standalone breakup

## Founder voice note script (~20 seconds)

Use on LinkedIn audio or a short Loom. Sound human, not rehearsed.

> "Hey {{FIRST}}, {{FOUNDER_NAME}} here - keeping this quick. You're putting a
> document assistant in front of regulated buyers, and the question that stalls
> those deals is 'prove it doesn't make things up, and show us the audit trail.'
> We turn every answer into a reproducible, tamper-evident receipt, running inside
> your environment. I'd love to show you ten minutes, and if it's useful, run a
> two-week pilot on your real answers. Reply if you're open to it - thanks!"

---

## Standalone breakup message (email or DM)

Short, no guilt, leaves the door open.

**Subject (if email):** closing the loop

> Hi {{FIRST}} - I'll stop reaching out so I'm not noise in your inbox. If the
> "prove your assistant is grounded" question ever comes up in a security review,
> reply here and I'll send a two-week pilot scope the same day. Good luck with the
> build. - {{FOUNDER_NAME}}
