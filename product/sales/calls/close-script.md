# Pilot close script

Use after the demo, once the buyer has acknowledged the pain. Ask for the money.

## The close

> "Based on what you've seen - if we ran this on ~200 of your real answers and the
> report surfaced fabricated citations or contradictions, would that change how your
> current deal review goes?"

(Wait for a yes. If it's a maybe, go back to pain, not features.)

> "Then here's what I'd propose. A two-week pilot. $2,500 fixed - and we have two
> design-partner slots at $1,500, credited toward your first three months if you
> continue. You give us ~200 real answers and the evidence each used, plus one
> technical contact. We deploy in your environment, tune a rule pack for your
> domain, and give you a report your prospect's auditor can reproduce. Can we start
> {{DAY}}?"

## Handling the common stalls

- **"Send me something I can review."** "Done - I'll send a one-page scope and a
  one-pager you can forward today. Can we hold {{DAY}} tentatively to start?"
- **"I need to check with {{PERSON}}."** "Makes sense. Want me to join a 15-minute
  call with them, or would the one-pager do it? Who else should see the report when
  it's done?"
- **"Let me think about it."** "Of course. What's the one thing you'd need to be
  true to say yes? I'd rather solve that now than chase you."

3. Deployment: in-VPC Docker / embedded SDK (pick one)
4. Domain rule pack: insurance_v1 / fintech_v1 / custom
5. Who sees the final report: ______
6. Start date: ______  ·  Review call: ______

> "I'll send the scope and a payment link within the hour. Looking forward to it."
